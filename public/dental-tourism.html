Dental Tourism in Kosovo — Klinika Dentare Medident, Pejë

Klinika Dentare Medident in Pejë, Kosovo welcomes international patients from the UK, Germany, Switzerland, Scandinavia, and the Balkans.

How It Works

  1. Send your scan — WhatsApp your OPG or CBCT to +383 49 772 307. Dr. Lendita reviews it personally.
  2. We contact you — A real team member calls or messages to discuss your case and plan.
  3. Come to Pejë — We pick you up from Prishtina airport. Treatment, recovery, Rugova Canyon.

Location

Pejë (Peja) is 1 hour from Prishtina International Airport (PRN). The clinic is on Rr. Bajram Kelmendi in the city centre. Partner accommodation: Hotel Dukagjini, Pejë. Recovery nearby: Rugova Canyon, 30 minutes from the clinic.

Treatments Available for International Patients

  • Dental implants (single, multiple, All-on-4/6/8)
  • Sinus lift and bone graft
  • Zirconia crowns
  • Full smile rehabilitation
