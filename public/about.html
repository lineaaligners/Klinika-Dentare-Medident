Klinika Dentare Medident — Dental Clinic in Pejë, Kosovo

Klinika Dentare Medident is a family dental clinic in Pejë (Peja), Kosovo, founded in 1999 by Dr. Lendita Islami Nallbani. The clinic has grown from a 16m² single-room practice to a 200m² full-service dental centre with 15 staff members and 13,000+ patients treated.
