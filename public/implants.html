Dental Implants in Pejë, Kosovo

Klinika Dentare Medident offers dental implant surgery in Pejë, Kosovo, led by Dr. Lendita Islami Nallbani — oral surgery specialist, implantologist, and the first woman in Kosovo to place a dental implant in 2004.

Implant Systems

  • Hiossen ET III (USA) — FDA-cleared, SA Surface treatment, tapered design, high primary stability
  • MegaGen AnyRidge (South Korea) — CE certified, XPEED Surface, KnifeThread design

Our Approach: 3D-Planned Guided Surgery

Every implant case at Medident begins with a CBCT 3D scan. Dr. Lendita plans the exact angle, depth, and diameter of each implant digitally before surgery. A 3D-printed surgical guide is made for that patient, so the implant is placed through a precise, pre-planned path — flapless when possible. Shorter surgery, less swelling, faster recovery.

Experience

Dr. Lendita Islami Nallbani has placed 4,000+ implants over 21 years of implantology practice. She reviews every incoming implant case personally before the patient commits to treatment.
